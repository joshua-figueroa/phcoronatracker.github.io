About

This website was created by Joshua Figueroa, a Grade 10 student from Cavite. The purpose of this website is to help people to get the latest and simplified information about the COVID-19 Pandemic in our country. This also aims to prevent the spread of fake news and panic. It includes a dashboard that tallies the total cases of the disease, maps about their residency and a graph that tells its trend. This site is constantly updated and is sourced from the Department of Health and other media outlets.
